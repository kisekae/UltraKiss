Welcome!

This is the UltraKiss helpful Tips system.

These tips will help you get started with UltraKiss. You can use UltraKiss to view all your KiSS dolls and even edit these dolls if so desired.

UltraKiss supports all FKiSS effects to FKiSS 4 on Windows, Mac OS X, and Linux platforms.

UltraKiss also implements many proposed FKiSS extensions discussed by the KiSS community.

To view online KiSS sets, use the UltraKiss Portal. Press the toolbar button with the Kanji icon
