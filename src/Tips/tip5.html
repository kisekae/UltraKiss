Thumbnails of Sets

Did you know that UltraKiss can automatically create thumbnail images of KiSS sets on your computer?
The Search module, invoked through the File-Search command, scan files in the directories and sub-directories looking for LZH and ZIP archives.

Any KiSS set will be catalogued and indexed for access through the UltraKiss Portal. A thumbnail image of the KiSS set will be automatically generated.

The search options are specified in Options-Search.
