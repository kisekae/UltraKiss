Debugging FKiSS

Have you ever wondered why FKiSS code did not work properly?
UltraKiss has a powerful FKiSS debugger, known as the FKiSS Editor tool. Press the Breakpoint FKiSS button on the toolbar to invoke the editor when the next event is fired.

The green indicator at the right end of the toolbar will change to yellow to indicate that the debugger is watching for an event.

When the next event happens, the FKiSS Editor will be automatically launched.  You can step through the code one statement at a time and watch it run.

You can also temporarily stop all FKiSS events from firing by pressing the indicator light at the end of the toolbar. This is a global switch to disable the system event handler.
